Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical response time?

We typically respond to inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, please use our Discord channel for faster support.

Do you offer enterprise support?

Yes, we offer dedicated enterprise support plans with priority response times and custom solutions. Contact our sales team for more information.

Where can I find documentation?

Our comprehensive documentation is available at docs.ollama.ai. It includes guides, API references, and examples.

How do I report a security issue?

For security-related issues, please email security@ollama.ai directly. Do not post security vulnerabilities in public forums or GitHub issues.
